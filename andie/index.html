Who is andie?

Discover the possibilities

ANDIE is a vision! ANDIE is growth! ANDIE if freedom! ANDIE is a new dawn. Do schools kill creativity? ANDIE summit is an event that will inspire and challenge you to think differently about education and start developing yourself for your future. Attending the summit will be over 3000 participants who will together contribute towards the paradigm shift in what we know and think of education

I want to register already

Why is ANDIE for you?

Make the best

Well, if you are interested in making the best of your learning experiences both on and off the walls of a classroom; we are certain that you should attend the ANDIE summit.

Engaging panelists

During the summit, the panelist and participants will engage in how to empower the youth to discover their passion and talents and even how to capitalize on these for personal growth. We envision a generation of out-of-the-box thinkers who can provide innovative solutions to some of the issues we face in the country.

Healthy discussions

Also, there will be healthy discussions on how the private sector can connect with institutions of higher learning to develop the studentsâ€™ employability by cultivating the skills required in the dynamic job market.

Awesome Venue

The event will be a perfect avenue for youth to identify and connect with potential mentors, share their innovative ideas and network with potential employers and business partners. There will be an exhibition running concurrently at the same venue for all interested innovators to showcase their products and link with the right people. Those interested in exhibiting their products or innovation are encouraged to contact summit@andie.or.ke for more information.
